Why ACH

Lower cost for you, simpler for everyone

We collect rent by ACH bank transfer instead of credit cards. Card processing adds 3%+ in fees — on a $1,800 rent that's over $50 every month. ACH keeps your cost to a small flat fee.

  • One low flat fee per payment — typically around $1–$3, not a percentage of your rent
  • Set up autopay so you never miss a due date or late fee
  • Bank-level security — your account is verified and encrypted
  • Instant emailed receipt and full payment history
Heads up: we don't accept credit or debit cards for rent. ACH is safer and far cheaper for tenants — the small flat fee covers processing only.
