Renter FAQ

Quick answers

How do I apply?

Use our online application. You'll fill it out, pay the fee, and complete screening securely — all in one flow.

Why is my SSN entered separately?

For your protection. You enter it directly with the screening bureau — we never see or store it.

Can I pay rent with a card?

We accept ACH bank transfer only — it's far cheaper for you, with just a small flat fee instead of 3%+ card fees.

What's an emergency?

Anything threatening safety or causing major damage — flooding, no heat/AC, gas smell, etc. Call our 24/7 line right away.
